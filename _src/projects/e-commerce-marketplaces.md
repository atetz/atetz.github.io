What if you could expand your e-commerce sales across a wide range of different channels and social platforms?

Platforms like _Channel Engine or Channable_ allow customers sell anywhere after a single setup. For a certain fee, you can list products on multiple market places and get advertising and analytics tools.

I have helped multiple e-commerce clients overcome this challenge by integrating their ERP system (SAP, Exact, Dynamics / Navision etc.) with these marketplace platforms.

The streams I've built:

- Platform orders to the ERP
- ERP status updates about shipments and inventory to the platform
- Platform returns to the ERP
- Product information syncronisation

As a result, the business scales up to multiple marketplaces and the technical landscape scales down to fewer integrations.
By using a centralized integration platform, I develop core components once and re-use them for every new channel. This directly reduces maintenance costs and allows for flexible scaling.
Do you want to add or change a system / component? Then only a certain part in the chain is affected.
